How to Translate PDF Files

Translating PDF files online is now very easy. You can upload a PDF document and convert it into another language within seconds.

PDF translators are useful for students, office work, ebooks, research papers, and international communication.

Steps to Translate a PDF

  1. Open the PDF translator website.
  2. Upload your PDF file.
  3. Select the original language.
  4. Choose the language you want to translate into.
  5. Click the “Translate” button.
  6. Download the translated PDF.
